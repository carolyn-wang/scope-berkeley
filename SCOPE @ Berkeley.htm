What is SCOPE?

SCOPE is a monthly virtual event that aims to help high school students discover their passions and curiosities. Each month, SCOPE invites a panel of professors from a wide variety of fields to talk about their research. There, students are exposed to a vast variety of problems and subjects to help them better determine their interests.
